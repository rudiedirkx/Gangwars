*  The charachters:
Hitman, Junkie, Computernerd, SWAT, Businessman. More info: Charachters

*  The map contains:   P = Pharmacie
R = Restaurant A = Arms 'r' us
S = Safehouse T = Telephone
/ = Unexplored H = Hospital
X = Empty Sector C = Community Centre
W = Way Out M = Mall (huge/medium/small)

*  You cant immediatly play after your signup. You'll have to be invited to enter a Gang. You can also start a new gang yourself; in that case, you are the gangleader! You will be allowed and accepted in the real game; this happens only if your/the gang is complete and the gangleader has accepted all gangsettings.

*  The stage before the actual game allows trading and moneymaking. You could get a job, offer your services somewhere, sabotage other gangs, etc. You could also gain some info on gangs and players already in the real game.
You can make a team with several gangs or individual players; this is unofficial though. Tips: Manual

*  General info:
This game is fast; faster than any; very fast. Every tick (mostlikely to be 30sec.) one turn will be added and interest will be calculated (Businessmen only). This speed can be in your advantage, if you play long and much (why not spend 60hours of your weekend?). It will be your disadvantage when you dont play for a day; you could be murdered, you could be robbed and be in a hospital (which will have to be paid).


*  Every gang has a Community Centre. It has to be conquered! Every map has 1 CC. The chance someone not in your gang sees your CC after its been taken by your gang is small. When noticed, it can be attacked (all members of the attacking gang must've seen the CC).

*  Every map has one Hospital. All the rest of the sectors are randomly divided. It could happen, one map without a Weaponsdealer anywhere; but it can also happen a map has 3 Safehouses.

*  Every player has a bagagelimit. This will be maximized by 3 factors:
max volume, max weight, max amount of articles.
These max values could differ per charachter. Some can be expanded

*  Every gang has its own colour and name. A gangleader can be appointed by its members. This, only with a 66.67% majority (thats 4/5 gangmembers). Default gangleader is the one who created the gang: its first member.
